## 🌿 What is SoulSync?

> *Most wellness apps are cluttered, cold, and clinical. SoulSync is different.*

SoulSync is a **mental wellness and emotional connection platform** designed to help users reflect on their emotions, track their mood, and maintain better mental health — wrapped in a clean, calming mobile experience.

```
  Reflect on your emotions  →  Track your mood  →  Gain personal insights
          └──────────────────────────┬────────────────────────┘
                                     │
                          Better mental wellness 🌱
```
